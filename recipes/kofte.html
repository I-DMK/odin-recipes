Turkish Köfte

Description

Köfte is a classic Turkish dish made from ground meat, typically beef or lamb, mixed with onions, herbs, and spices. The mixture is shaped into small patties or meatballs and either grilled or pan-fried. Köfte is perfect for lunch, served alongside rice, pita bread, a fresh salad, and yogurt for a complete and satisfying meal.

This recipe is easy to prepare and allows for flavor customization with spices like cumin, paprika, and parsley, giving the köfte a delicious, aromatic taste. It’s a versatile dish that can also be served in sandwiches or wraps.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef or lamb
  • 1 medium onion, finely grated
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground paprika
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 egg (optional, for binding)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil for frying
  • Rice, salad, and yogurt for serving

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground meat, grated onion, garlic, parsley, cumin, paprika, black pepper, and salt. Mix well to incorporate all ingredients evenly.
  2. If desired, add an egg to the mixture for additional binding. Mix until the ingredients form a cohesive meat mixture.
  3. Shape the meat mixture into small patties or oval-shaped meatballs (about 2 inches in diameter).
  4. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the köfte patties in batches, making sure not to overcrowd the pan.
  5. Cook each side for about 3-4 minutes or until the köfte is browned and cooked through.
  6. Serve the köfte hot with rice, salad, and a side of yogurt. Enjoy!
